About Me

I'm currently working on my thesis, using a deep learning automated system to detect stress from ECG data. It was during my master program that I discovered how much I liked artificial intelligence and computer science combined, and I would like to keep expanding my knowledge on this matter.

My master program in Medical Informatics focused on coding and classification systems of diseases and procedures, image processing, and the implementation and evaluation of information systems.

During this time I also published the paper Vieira, E. et al (2021) Data Mining Approach to Classify Cases of Lung Cancer .

I have experience with programming languages such as Python and Java, machine learning platforms such as Tensorflow, and web design skills, including HTML and CSS. I also have a practical knowledge of SQL and database concepts and solid knowledge of cybersecurity principles.
